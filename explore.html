Tropical North Queensland

Gateway to Cairns & Beyond

Ahana Hillside sits along the Bruce Highway at Aloomba, just 35 minutes south of Cairns — the gateway to Tropical North Queensland. From here, two UNESCO World Heritage sites are at your doorstep: the Great Barrier Reef and the Daintree Rainforest, along with hidden waterfalls, volcanic crater lakes, and some of Australia's most scenic drives.

Cairns itself is a relaxed, tropical city with a vibrant waterfront, free saltwater lagoon, weekend markets, and world-class dining. Whether you're chasing waterfalls at Josephine Falls, snorkelling the outer reef, exploring the ancient Daintree, or simply browsing Rusty's Markets for tropical fruit — everything is within easy reach.

Whether you're here for adventure or simply to soak in the surroundings, there's something remarkable in every direction. Ahana Hillside is your base to experience it all.
