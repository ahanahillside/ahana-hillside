About Us

Our story and what makes Ahana special

Our Story

Ahana Hillside is a private retreat nestled in the lush tropical rainforest hills just outside Cairns, in Tropical North Queensland. What began as a love for the land has grown into a welcoming space where travellers can disconnect from the everyday and reconnect with nature.

Set on a quiet hillside surrounded by native bushland, our property offers a peaceful escape with sweeping views and the sounds of the rainforest. Whether you're looking for a budget-friendly stay or a unique camping experience, Ahana Hillside is your base to explore everything the region has to offer.

The Experience

Wake up to birdsong and morning mist rolling through the hills. Spend your days exploring the Great Barrier Reef, Daintree Rainforest, or the Atherton Tablelands, and return to the tranquillity of your hillside retreat.

Our eco-friendly campsites are designed to sit gently on the land, offering a genuine connection to the tropical environment while providing the essentials for a comfortable stay.
