Welcome

Eco-Friendly Camping & Stays Near Cairns

Tucked away in the lush green hills just 35 minutes south of Cairns, Ahana Hillside is a private rainforest retreat where nature meets comfort. Wake to birdsong, breathe in the tropical air, and let the pace of the hillside slow you down.

Whether you're camping under the stars at one of our spacious campsites or enjoying a budget-friendly room, Ahana is your base to explore the wonders of Tropical North Queensland — from the Great Barrier Reef to ancient Daintree Rainforest and hidden waterfalls.

The Neighbourhood

Things to Do Near Cairns & the Great Barrier Reef

Nestled in the hills above Cairns, Ahana Hillside places you at the doorstep of Tropical North Queensland's most extraordinary natural wonders. From ancient rainforests and cascading waterfalls to the Great Barrier Reef, this corner of Australia offers experiences found nowhere else on Earth.

Spend your mornings chasing waterfalls at Crystal Cascades or Josephine Falls, your afternoons snorkelling the reef, and your evenings watching the sunset from your hillside retreat.
