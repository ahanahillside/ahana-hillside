The Retreat

A place apart

Ahana Hillside sits on a gentle slope overlooking open farmland and native bushland. There are no neighbors in sight — just birdsong, shifting light, and the kind of quiet that lets your thoughts settle.

This is a whole-property rental, meaning the entire retreat is yours alone. Whether you're here as a couple, a small family, or on your own, you'll have complete privacy and the freedom to use the space however feels right.

We built Ahana as a place we'd want to return to ourselves — considered, comfortable, and deeply connected to the landscape around it.

Accommodation

The spaces

Ahana Hillside is a single, self-contained dwelling designed for two to four guests.

Inside, you'll find an open living and kitchen area with north-facing windows, one spacious bedroom with a king bed, and a second flexible room with a daybed that can serve as a reading nook or second sleeping space. The bathroom is simple and generous, with a rainfall shower and good natural light.

Outside, a wide timber deck wraps around two sides of the house. It's where most guests end up spending their mornings — coffee in hand, watching the valley wake up.

The Experience

What it feels like

The first thing you notice is the quiet. Not silence exactly — there's wind through the trees, the occasional call of a currawong — but a stillness that lets you hear yourself think again.

Mornings here have a particular quality. The light comes in soft and golden, the air is cool and clean, and there's no urgency to be anywhere or do anything. Afternoons stretch out. Evenings come slowly.

"Guests often tell us they sleep better here than they have in months."

This isn't a place for adventure or entertainment. It's a place to land, to rest, and to remember what it feels like to have time.
