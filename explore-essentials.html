Fully Equipped Kitchen

Nearby

Shops & Dining

When you need to stock up on groceries or grab a quick bite, everything is a short drive down the Bruce Highway.

IGA Supermarket

Closest store to Ahana Hillside

Your nearest supermarket for all everyday essentials. Well-stocked with fresh produce, pantry staples, camping supplies, and everything you need to make the most of the on-site kitchen. Conveniently open until midnight for those late-night needs or if you forgot something for tomorrow's breakfast.

Open until midnight Fresh produce · Pantry · Camping supplies

Woolworths Gordonvale

~10 min north on Bruce Highway

A full-range Woolworths supermarket in Gordonvale with everything from fresh groceries and bakery items to household goods and pharmacy essentials. If you're planning to cook up a storm in the Ahana kitchen, this is the place to do a proper shop on your way in.

~10 min drive Full grocery · Bakery · Pharmacy

Fast Food & Dining

~10 min in Gordonvale area

Craving a quick bite? KFC and McDonald's are both within easy reach in the Gordonvale area, along with a selection of local cafes and eateries perfect for a relaxed meal out. Great for those nights when you'd rather let someone else do the cooking.

~10 min drive KFC · McDonald's · Local cafes
