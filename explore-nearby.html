What's Nearby

Explore the best of Tropical North Queensland

World Heritage

Great Barrier Reef

Just off the coast lies the world's largest coral reef system — a kaleidoscope of marine life, vibrant coral gardens, and crystal-clear waters. Whether you snorkel, dive, or explore by glass-bottom boat, the reef is an unforgettable experience and one of the main reasons visitors come to Tropical North Queensland.

Day trips depart daily from Cairns Marina · ~45 min by boat
Ancient Rainforest

Daintree Rainforest

The Daintree is the oldest continuously surviving tropical rainforest on Earth — over 180 million years old. Drive north through lush canopy, cross the Daintree River by ferry, and discover Cape Tribulation where the rainforest meets the reef. Walking trails, wildlife spotting, and pristine beaches await.

~2 hours drive north of Ahana Hillside
Natural Wonders

Waterfalls & Swimming Holes

Tropical North Queensland is home to some of Australia's most beautiful waterfalls and natural swimming holes. Cool off at Crystal Cascades, marvel at the tiered Josephine Falls, swim among the ancient Babinda Boulders, or photograph the iconic curtain of Millaa Millaa Falls surrounded by lush rainforest.

Crystal Cascades ~20 min · Josephine Falls ~1 hr · Babinda Boulders ~50 min
Highlands

Atherton Tablelands

Rise above the coast to the cool, green highlands of the Atherton Tablelands. Discover volcanic crater lakes like Lake Barrine and Lake Eacham, walk through the curtain of the famous Cathedral Fig Tree, spot wild platypus at dawn, and wander the charming village of Yungaburra with its heritage markets and relaxed pace.

~1 hour drive from Ahana Hillside via Gillies Highway
Coastal

Beaches & Islands

From the palm-lined shores of Palm Cove and the family-friendly sands of Trinity Beach to the coral-fringed Fitzroy Island and the tropical paradise of Green Island, the coastline near Cairns offers something for every beach lover. Swim, kayak, snorkel, or simply relax with views across the Coral Sea.

Palm Cove ~25 min · Fitzroy Island ~45 min by ferry
The City

Cairns City

Cairns is the vibrant gateway to Tropical North Queensland. Stroll along the Esplanade and take a dip in the free public Lagoon, browse the fresh produce and local crafts at Rusty's Markets, or enjoy waterfront dining along the Boardwalk. With a laid-back tropical atmosphere and plenty to see and do, the city makes for a great day out.

~35 min drive from Ahana Hillside
